PRIVACY POLICY

PLEASE READ

This is the privacy policy of Blue North Sustainability Proprietary Limited ("us", "we", or "our") and applies to all our Platforms and Products.

“Platforms” means all our websites, mobile sites, social media platforms or any other technology or mechanism you may use to interact with us;

“Resources” means downloadable documentation, tools and/or resources that we make available to you as supporting resources to our Software;

“Products” means both our Software and our Resources; and

“Software” means our software, including our online web applications, tools, software based services, mobile applications or any other technology that you selected and that we may license to you.

This privacy policy describes how we handle the information we collect and receive from you. By using our Platforms or Products you agree to the provisions of this privacy policy.

  1. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

    We may collect different types of information from you in the following ways:

    1. Information you give us

      While using our Platforms or Products, we may ask you to provide us with certain information about you. This includes any information (including personal information) that you provide to us directly, whether through our Platforms or Products or via phone, text messages, social media, fax or any other medium. This includes, but is not limited to, information you provide to us:

      1. by filling in forms on our Platforms or Products (when you sign-up for an account or when you subscribe or register to use services on our Platforms or Products);
      2. when you enter a competition, promotion or complete a survey;
      3. by posting comments or content on our Platforms or Products;
      4. when you purchase one of our Products;
      5. when you contact us and when you otherwise provide information directly to us; or
      6. when you use our Platforms or Products.

    2. Information we collect or receive when you use our Platforms or Products

      We collect information when you use our Platforms or Products through various means, this may include by using cookies, web beacons and other tracking technologies. You can manage or disable cookies in your browser settings. Depending on how you access and use our Platforms or Products, we may receive or collect:

      1. Log information (information about your interactions with our Platforms or Products and other services you use, the time and date of your visit, the time spend on a page, the pages you visit, or the content you view and the search queries you submit);
      2. Information we infer about you based on your interaction with our Platforms or Products;
      3. Device information (the type of device you're using, how you access our Platforms or Products, your browser type, browser version, or operating system, diagnostic data and your Internet Protocol ("IP") address);
      4. Location information (your device's GPS signal and information about nearby WiFi networks and cell towers). We get this information when you use location- enabled services. You can disable location services when you use or Platforms or Products, through your device settings;
      5. Data about you or your business, including but not limited to, carbon emission or carbon footprint related data (primary data for the purpose of calculating product carbon footprint), data that will allow us to determine patterns, trends and/or risks in an industry; and
      6. Google Analytics (website traffic data – also refer to clause 3.2.6 below).

    3. Information from third-party sources

      We may receive additional information about you that is publicly or commercially available and combine that with the information we have collected or received about you in other ways. We may also receive information about you when you choose to connect with social networking services while using our Platforms or Products.

  2. HOW WE USE THE INFORMATION WE COLLECT AND RECEIVE

    We use the information we collect and receive for the following general purposes:

    1. to provide you with information, products or services you request from us;
    2. to improve and maintain our Platforms or Products;
    3. to be effective and relevant in the services we provide you;
    4. to provide you with support;
    5. to communicate with you;
    6. to allow you to participate in interactive features of our Platforms or Products when you choose to do so;
    7. to monitor the usage of our Platforms or Products;
    8. to provide effective advertising (provide you with news, special offers and general information about other goods, services and events which we offer, that are similar to those that you have already purchased or enquired about); and
    9. to create anonymized databases and reports, including but not limited to, industry benchmarking and other aggregated reports, trend/pattern reports, risk profile reports, all of which we may provide or sell to our customers or other third parties.

  3. HOW WE SHARE THE INFORMATION WE COLLECT AND RECEIVE

    1. We don't sell your personal information to third parties.
    2. We may share other information with:
      1. our affiliates, in other words, other companies in our group;
      2. third parties, where we share anonymized data and reports (we share non- personally identifiable information (anonymized data) with other customers or third parties, by using the data we collected (not the personal information) to create aggregated and anonymized reports. As an example, we will collate the carbon footprint data we collect into industry benchmarking reports. We may share or sell this data and/or reports to third parties. Such data and/or reports are always anonymized and we never share personal or identifiable information about you in our reports;
      3. other parties in response to legal process, laws, or when necessary to conduct or protect our legal rights;
      4. other parties in connection with certain business transactions (in the event that we restructure or sell any of our businesses or assets, we may disclose your personal information to the prospective buyer of such business or assets or other transacting party);
      5. companies that provide services to us (companies that provide services to us or act on our behalf may have access to information about you. These companies are limited in their ability to use information they receive in the course of providing services to us or you);
      6. Google Analytics (Google Analytics is a web analytics service offered by Google that tracks and reports website traffic. Google uses the data collected to track and monitor the use of our Platforms or Products. This data is shared with other Google services. Google may use the collected data to contextualize and personalize the ads of its own advertising network. You can opt-out of Google Analytics by installing the Google Analytics opt-out browser add-on. The add-on prevents the Google Analytics JavaScript (ga.js, analytics.js, and dc.js) from sharing information with Google Analytics about your activities. For more information on the privacy practices of Google, visit the Google Privacy Terms web page: http://www.google.com/intl/en/policies/privacy/).

  4. YOUR CHOICES

    1. You have the right to ask us not to contact you for marketing purposes. You can exercise this right at any time by using any of the various "opt-out" options that we will always provide to you when we communicate with you. We won't send you marketing messages if you tell us not to but we will still need to send you service-related messages.
    2. We want to make sure that any data we hold about you is up to date. So if you think your personal information is inaccurate, you can ask us to correct or remove it.
    3. You can also update your personal information directly within your account settings section. If you are unable to change your personal information, please contact us to make the required changes.
    4. If you wish to be informed what personal information we hold about you and if you want it to be removed from our systems, please contact us.

  5. MONITORING OF COMMUNICATIONS

    We may monitor and record our communications with you, including e-mails and phone conversations. Information which we collect may then be used for training purposes, quality assurance, to record details about the products and services you use or ask us about, and in order to meet our legal and regulatory obligations generally.

    1. We will retain your personal information only for as long as is necessary for the purposes set out in this privacy policy or to comply with our legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our legal agreements and policies.
    2. We may retain other data for internal analysis purposes.

  7. TRANSFER OF DATA

    1. We are based in and operate from South Africa. Your information, including personal information, may be transferred to and maintained on servers located outside of your country, where the data privacy laws, regulations and standards, may not be equivalent to the laws in your country of residence.
    2. We might transfer your personal information to places outside of South Africa and store it there, where our suppliers might process it. If that happens, we require our suppliers to apply the same security standards as when we process your information in South Africa.
    3. Your consent to this privacy policy followed by your submission of such information to us, represents your agreement to such transfer.
    4. We will take all steps reasonably necessary to ensure that your data is treated securely and in accordance with this privacy policy.

  8. SECURITY OF DATA

    The security of your data is important to us. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security. However, we do employ a number of safeguards intended to mitigate the risk of unauthorized access or disclosure of your information. We will do our best to protect your personal information and we will use up to date technology that will help us to do this. We will at all times comply with our obligation under law.

    1. We may provide paid products and/or services on our Platforms or Products. We use third- party services for payment processing.
    2. We will not store or collect your payment card details. That information is provided directly to our third-party payment processors whose use of your personal information is governed by their privacy policy. These payment processors adhere to the standards set by PCI-DSS as managed by the PCI Security Standards Council, which is a joint effort of brands like Visa, Mastercard, American Express and Discover. PCI-DSS requirements help ensure the secure handling of payment information.
    3. The payment processor we work with is Payfast. Their privacy policy can be viewed at https://www.payfast.co.za/privacy-policy/.

  11. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

    1. Our Platforms or Products does not address anyone under the age of 18 ("Children").
    2. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from anyone under the age of 18. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your Children have provided us with personal information, please contact us. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from Children without verification of parental consent, we take steps to remove that information from our Platforms or Products.

    1. We may update our privacy policy from time to time. Any changes to this privacy policy will be posted on our Platforms.
    2. You are advised to visit our website periodically and review our privacy policy. Changes to our privacy policy are effective from when they are posted on our Platforms.
